The Best Movies According to Rajalakshmi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Ennu Ninte Moideen

It is based on the real-life tragic love story of Moideen and Kanchanamala, which took place in the 1960s in Mukkam, a village in Kerala.The film portrays a powerful love story between the protagonists, Moideen and Kanchanamala. The enduring love despite societal opposition and challenges touches the hearts of viewers.

Everything Everywhere All at Once

This film is the most thrilling film that i have ever watched.This film's inventive storytelling and exploration of parallel universes certainly make for an exciting and thought-provoking experience.

Megan

"Megan" could serve as a cautionary tale or speculative fiction narrative that delves into the potential consequences of AI technology on society, ethics, and human relationships.
